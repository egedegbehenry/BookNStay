Single Room

A single room is designed to accommodate one person. It typically features a single bed and provides essential amenities for a comfortable stay.
It has One single bed and generally the smallest room type and it have the following basic amenities such as a writing desk, a small wardrobe, a television, and a private bathroom with essential toiletries.
The view is cool and it has a great view of the city and courtyard.

King Room

A king room offers more space and features a large king-size bed, ideal for couples or solo travelers who prefer extra sleeping space.
It have one king-size bed larger than a single room, offering more living space and great amenities such as comfortable seating area, a larger wardrobe, a writing desk, a flat-screen TV, coffee/tea making facilities, and a private bathroom with upgraded toiletries The view is the cityscape, pool, and garden

Queen Room

Similar to a king room but with a queen-size bed, suitable for couples or individuals who don't need as much space as a king bed offers.
It have one king-size bed larger than a single room, offering more living space and great amenities such as comfortable seating area, a larger wardrobe, a writing desk, a flat-screen TV, coffee/tea making facilities, and a private bathroom with upgraded toiletries The view is the cityscape, pool, and garden.

Deluxe Room

A deluxe room provides a more luxurious experience with added space and upscale furnishings. Bed: One king-size or two queen-size beds Size: Larger than single rooms, offering more living space and comfort Amenities: High-quality furnishings, sitting area, large wardrobe, writing desk, flat-screen TV, minibar, premium coffee/tea making facilities, and a private bathroom with luxury toiletries and possibly a bathtub View: Often features premium views, such as ocean, mountains, and landmarks.

Executive Suite

Executive rooms are designed for business travelers, providing extra space, enhanced amenities, and sometimes access to exclusive facilities. Bed: One king-size bed and one queen-size bed. Size: Larger than deluxe rooms, with added space for work and relaxation. Amenities: High-end furnishings, ergonomic work desk, comfortable seating area, flat-screen TV, minibar, premium coffee/tea making facilities, upgraded bathroom amenities, and often a bathtub. View: Premium views; and other benefits such as: access to executive lounges offering complimentary breakfast, snacks, and drinks.

Presidential Suite

The presidential suite represents the epitome of luxury in a hotel, offering expansive space, top-tier amenities, and exclusive services. Bed: One or more king-size beds. Size: The largest and most luxurious accommodation in the hotel, often including multiple rooms such as a bedroom, living room, dining area, and possibly a kitchen Amenities: Opulent furnishings, large living area, dining table, multiple flat-screen TVs, premium sound system, full minibar, coffee/tea making facilities, multiple bathrooms with high-end toiletries, jacuzzi or hot tub, and possibly a private balcony or terrace View: The best views the hotel has to offer, such as panoramic cityscape, ocean, or mountains; it has other benefits and it include personalized services such as a private butler, concierge service, and access to exclusive hotel facilities
