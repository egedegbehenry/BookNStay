Deluxe Suite

Welcome to our Deluxe Suite, where luxury meets comfort. This spacious suite features an elegant living area with plush sofas and a flat-screen TV, a stylish dining area, and a fully-equipped kitchen with modern appliances.
The luxurious bedroom boasts a king-sized bed with premium linens and a second flat-screen TV. The en-suite bathroom offers a deep soaking tub, walk-in rain shower, and premium toiletries. Enjoy stunning views from your private balcony. Additional amenities include a dedicated workspace, high-speed internet, climate control, an in-room safe, and daily housekeeping.
Guests have access to exclusive hotel facilities, including a rooftop pool, fitness center, spa, fine dining restaurants, and 24-hour concierge service.
Experience unparalleled luxury and convenience in our Deluxe Suite.
